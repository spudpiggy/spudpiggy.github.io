These jars should go into your mods folder.

Vanilla Launcher users

Your mods folder will be in %appdata%/.minecraft/mods if you use Windows.

It's recommended to make a 1.16.5 folder within your mods folder and extract this modpack to that.

This way you can have multiple modpacks for different game versions.

MultiMC users

If you use MultiMC, the mods folder can be easily accessed if you highlight your Forge 1.16.5 instance and click "Instance Folder" and go into your mods folder.

You do not need version folders on MultiMC, as each instance essentially has a separate .minecraft folder.
